How It Works


Companies post projects for real business challenges they are facing.

 

Students apply with a team to projects they are intrested in or form a team on the site.

 

Student teams work on the project with ongoing support and ineraction with the company.

 

Innovative solutions and ideas are presented according to challenge criteria.

 

The company selects winning team, and offer feedback, interviews, and awards.
